2.4 · Analysing Text at Scale

How do you actually analyse thousands of prompts? Text, not numbers.

01
Embedding clustering
Vectorise prompts. Let similar questions find each other. The clusters become your taxonomy — discovered, not assumed.
Analytics analogue: segmentation
02
LLM-as-judge
Use a model to categorise and score other outputs. Run it as a batch over the trace history. Cheap labelling at scale.
Analytics analogue: custom dimensions
03
Feedback loops
Capture thumbs / ratings / corrections at the trace. Feed them back into evals so the next deploy is measurably better.
Analytics analogue: conversion events

Sound familiar? It's funnels and segments. Just different raw material.
